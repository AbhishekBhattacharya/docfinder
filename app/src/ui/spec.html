Dermatologist

A dermatologist is the medical expert you should consult if you have any significant problem with your skin. Dermatology is the science that is concerned with the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the skin, hair and nails.

Fees :-INR 500/-

Pediatrician

Pediatricians are doctors who manage the health of your child, including physical, behavior, and mental health issues. They're trained to diagnose and treat childhood illnesses, from minor health problems to serious diseases.

Fees :-INR 500/-

Gynecologist

Gynaecology or gynecology (see spelling differences) is the medical practice dealing with the health of the female reproductive systems (vagina, uterus, and ovaries) and the breasts. Literally, outside medicine, the term means "the science of women".

Fees :-INR 500/-

Opthalmologist

An ophthalmologist is a doctor whose specialty is diseases and conditions of the eyes, especially things that relate to vision. They are the doctors who examine your eyes and writes the prescription for your contact lenses.

Fees :-INR 500/-

Psychiatrist

A physiatrist is a medical doctor or doctor of osteopathic medicine who specializes in Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation (also called PM&R physicians). Physiatrists diagnose and treat both acute pain and chronic pain and specialize in a wide variety of nonsurgical treatments for the musculoskeletal system.

Fees :-INR 500/-

Dentist

A licensed practitioner who is skilled in the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of diseases, injuries, and malformations of the teeth, jaws, and mouth and who makes and inserts false teeth.

Fees :-INR 500/-

Cardiologist

A cardiologist is a heart doctor. He or she is the one to visit if you feel a tightness in your chest and shortness of breath.

  • slurred speech
  • a stuffy nose

Fees :-INR 500/-
